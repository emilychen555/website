Emily Y. Chen

I am a first year Master's student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (Intelligent Systems, Robotics, and Control track) at the University of California, San Diego advised by Dr. Xiaolong Wang. My current research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision and robotics.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Ilinois Urbana Champaign, advised by Dr. Shenlong Wang, where my senior thesis was on building a unified multi-modal representation for autonomous driving tasks.

I've also done optical quantum information research, specifically in developing a quantum memory system, with Dr. Paul Kwiat's group.
